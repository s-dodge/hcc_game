- Item.state: used for workstation (powered) and usb_drive (used) state tracking
- use [item] on [target] mechanic:

- Cinematic intro block in run(): narrative beats, sets visited=True, shows items/exits manually
- Typewriter effect: typewrite(text, char_delay=0.03, line_delay=0.4)

- help_desk_office added as real starting room
- all_rooms and all_items dicts in rooms.py and items.py respectively
- from items import * in rooms.py (pragmatic for small game)
