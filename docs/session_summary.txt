STORY PREMISE
-------------
Player is generic IT staff. Receives a ServiceDesk ticket (urgent, passive-aggressive tone).
Opening: rainy gray morning, player in help desk office, ticket appears on screen.
Steps out to find the building empty — flickering lights, cryptic note(s) planned.
Investigates. Finds badge (belongs to missing intern) + broken workstation = inciting clue.
USB drive in storage room contains a disturbing file (zalgo-corrupted eldritch text).
IT building is tutorial. Library (LRC) and Theatre are main game.
Tone: classic straight horror. The comedy is the mundane community college container — horror content is
never winking or ironic. Cognitive dissonance does the work.
Story draft in docs/story_notes.txt.
